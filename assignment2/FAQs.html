FAQs

General

What is this platform? A place to connect, collaborate, and study together online.

How do I join a study group? Sign up, browse groups, and request to join or create your own.

Is it free? Yes! Some premium features may be available.

Technical Support

Forgot password? Click “Forgot Password” on the login page.

Audio/video issues? Check browser permissions and try refreshing.

Study Groups

Can I create a group? Yes, you can start and manage your own study group.

How to remove a member? Group admins can manage and remove disruptive users.

Privacy & Safety

Is my data safe? Yes, we do not share personal information.

How to report inappropriate behavior? Use the “Report” button or contact support.
